Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How fast can you respond to an emergency?

We aim to reach you within 30–60 minutes. Call us and we'll give you an estimated arrival time.

Do you charge extra for after-hours service?

After-hours calls may have an additional fee. We'll always tell you the total cost before we begin.

Can you program keys for any car?

We work on virtually all makes and models, domestic and foreign, with advanced programming equipment.

Re-keying vs. replacing a lock?

Re-keying changes internal pins so old keys no longer work — more affordable. Replacing installs new hardware, better if worn or damaged.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Fully licensed, insured, and screened and approved on HomeAdvisor.

Do you offer free estimates?

Absolutely. Call or fill out the form. No obligation, no pressure.
